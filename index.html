Frequently Asked Questions

What is Nix Daily?

Nix Daily is an online sharing platform that provides you with various informative content every day such as music videos, healthcare advice, daily challenges, gaming, inspiration, and others.

When was Nix Daily founded?

Nix Daily was founded on May 13, 2020 in the Philippines.

How did Nix Daily started?

When the coronavirus outbreak hit the country and the government issued an enhanced community quarantine, we have decided to start the business to share news and updates related to COVID-19. As time goes by, we expand our scope of topics that we share everyday.
